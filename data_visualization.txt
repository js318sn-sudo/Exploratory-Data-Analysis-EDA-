Data Visualization

The dataset was visualized using different graphical techniques to understand patterns, relationships, and distributions in the data.

Visualizations Included:

* Histogram
* Boxplot
* Correlation Heatmap
* Countplot
* Pairplot

These visualizations helped in identifying:

* data distribution
* correlation between variables
* department-wise employee count
* trends in salary and experience
* relationships between numerical columns
